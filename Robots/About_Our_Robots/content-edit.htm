About Our Robots


Build

As a FIRST Robotics Competition team, we design and create a new robot every year based on the rules of the competition game that year. Beginning in early January every year, we work through 6 straight weeks of build season to analyze the game, prototype and design robot sub-assemblies, machine our parts from scratch, assemble all structural and electrical components, and program our robot. Through the next 7 weeks of competition season, we construct a second robot for practice, and continue making improvements to our hardware and software

Compete

The official FIRST competition season lasts for 7 weeks, over the course which we attend 2 regional competitions, and finally the World Championship at the end of the 7 weeks when we qualify. Out of the 5 years of our existence, we have made it to Worlds in the last 4 years, which is an amazing achievement for even teams that have existed for much longer.

Inspire

After 4 hectic months of competition season, we demo our robot at many public events, and train new students on the art of robot design and construction. We also attend some off-season competitions where newer members can gain more experience and exposure to FIRST robots.

We also help out many FIRST Lego League teams with constructing their robots, and outreach to newer teams to teach and to inspire.
